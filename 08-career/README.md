# Career Prompts

Prompts for resume rewrites, interview prep, case studies, and networking.

---

## AI PM Portfolio Bullet Rewriter

**Use case:** Rewrite resume bullets to show real AI product impact.
**Input needed:** Current bullets, project context, outcomes, tools used.

---

Rewrite my resume bullets for AI PM roles.

Rules:
- Keep each bullet under 28 words.
- Start with action verb.
- Include measurable impact where available.
- Show decision ownership, not task ownership.

Return 2 versions per bullet: conservative and bold.

---

**Tips:**
- Mention evaluation/quality ownership for AI features; recruiters scan for this.
- Avoid listing tools unless tied to outcomes.

## Case Study Storyline Builder

**Use case:** Prepare compelling product case studies for interviews.
**Input needed:** Project summary, problem, decisions, metrics, setbacks.

---

Build an interview-ready case study storyline.

Structure:
1. Context
2. Problem and stakes
3. Key decisions made
4. Tradeoffs and why
5. Outcome and metrics
6. What I would do differently

Keep it concise but specific.

---

**Tips:**
- Include one hard tradeoff to demonstrate PM judgment.
- Quantify impact and uncertainty separately.

## AI PM Interview Question Simulator

**Use case:** Practice for AI PM interviews with realistic pressure questions.
**Input needed:** Role JD, company stage, product domain, your experience level.

---

Simulate an AI PM interview panel.

Generate:
- 15 high-quality questions
- What a strong answer includes
- Common weak-answer traps
- 5 follow-up challenge questions

Bias toward product judgment, evaluation, and execution tradeoffs.

---

**Tips:**
- Practice verbally with a timer; written answers can mask weak reasoning.
- Use your own product examples instead of hypothetical answers.

## Networking Message Personalizer

**Use case:** Write non-generic outreach messages to PM/founder operators.
**Input needed:** Target person's profile, shared context, your ask, credibility signal.

---

Write 3 personalized outreach messages:
1. Warm intro version
2. Cold outreach version
3. Follow-up after no response

Each message must be under 120 words and include a clear, respectful ask.

---

**Tips:**
- Reference one specific point from their work to avoid generic outreach.
- Ask for one focused input, not broad career mentoring.

## 90-Day AI PM Learning Plan

**Use case:** Build an execution-first learning roadmap for PMs moving into AI.
**Input needed:** Current skill level, weekly hours, target role, product domain.

---

Create a 90-day AI PM learning plan.

Output format:
- Week-by-week plan
- Skills to build each phase
- Hands-on artifact to produce every 2 weeks
- Checkpoints to measure progress
- Final portfolio output

Prioritize shipping artifacts over passive reading.

---

**Tips:**
- Publish progress artifacts publicly to create accountability.
- Include at least one evaluation-focused artifact, not only prototypes.
