# Stakeholder Prompts

Prompts for executive updates, alignment, objection handling, and conflict resolution.

---

## Executive Update Composer

**Use case:** Share concise weekly updates with leadership.
**Input needed:** Progress, blockers, metrics, decisions needed.

---

Write an executive update using this format:
- This week: key progress
- Metrics movement
- Risks/blockers
- Decisions needed from leadership
- Next week focus

Tone: concise, no jargon, no defensive language.

---

**Tips:**
- Keep the "decisions needed" section explicit and actionable.
- Use trend lines, not one-off metric snapshots.

---

## Stakeholder Alignment Map

**Use case:** Plan influence strategy before a contentious product decision.
**Input needed:** Stakeholder list, priorities, objections, decision timeline.

---

Create a stakeholder alignment map.

For each stakeholder include:
- Their likely position
- Their top concern
- What evidence they trust
- Suggested message angle
- Ideal timing/channel

Then propose a sequencing plan for alignment conversations.

---

**Tips:**
- Start with high-influence skeptics early.
- Tailor evidence type (user quotes, numbers, risk controls) by stakeholder.

---

## Objection Handling Prep

**Use case:** Prepare for tough review meetings.
**Input needed:** Proposal summary, known objections, context from past reviews.

---

Simulate a critical review panel and generate:
1. Top 10 likely objections
2. Best response for each
3. Evidence needed to support each response
4. Which objections indicate real risk vs preference conflict

Keep responses honest and data-backed.

---

**Tips:**
- Pre-wire responses with relevant leads before the main meeting.
- Admit uncertainty where evidence is thin.

---

## Cross-Functional Decision Memo

**Use case:** Drive decision clarity across product, design, and engineering.
**Input needed:** Decision topic, options, constraints, recommendation.

---

Write a cross-functional decision memo with:
- Decision statement
- Context and constraints
- Options considered
- Recommendation
- Impact by function (Eng/Design/Data/GTM)
- Risks and mitigations
- Final ask and decision deadline

Keep it under 800 words.

---

**Tips:**
- Use this as a pre-read at least 24 hours before decision sync.
- Add an explicit fallback option if recommendation is rejected.

---

## Conflict-to-Resolution Facilitator

**Use case:** Resolve recurring team conflict around priorities or scope.
**Input needed:** Conflict context, stakeholder positions, constraints, deadlines.

---

Act as a neutral facilitator.

Provide:
1. Root conflict diagnosis
2. Shared goals all sides agree on
3. Negotiation options with tradeoffs
4. Recommended resolution path
5. Meeting agenda to close decision in one session

Prioritize business outcomes and team trust.

---

**Tips:**
- Focus on decision criteria before debating solutions.
- Document agreements and unresolved points in real time.
